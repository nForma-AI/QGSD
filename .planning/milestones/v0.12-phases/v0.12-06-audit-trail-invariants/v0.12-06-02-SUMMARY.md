# v0.12-06 Plan 02 — Alloy Formal Specs

## What Was Built

Authored two Alloy 6 formal specifications:

1. `.formal/alloy/scoreboard-recompute.als` (GAP-3) — machine-checked proof that the JS reset-then-replay scoreboard algorithm is idempotent, loses no votes, and counts no vote twice
2. `.formal/alloy/availability-parsing.als` (GAP-9) — proof that the date parser always returns a future timestamp or null, correctly handles year rollover, and never crashes on unrecognized input

## Assertions

### scoreboard-recompute.als (GAP-3)
- `RecomputeIdempotent` — computing score twice yields same result (deterministic pure function)
- `NoVoteLoss` — every vote's delta is reflected in the total sum
- `NoDoubleCounting` — score equals sum of individual round deltas, not more
- `TNplusScoreIsCorrect` — TN+ vote in a single round gives score 7 (sanity check)

### availability-parsing.als (GAP-9)
- `ParseCorrect` — parsed timestamp >= now (always future result)
- `YearRolloverHandled` — month earlier than current month still produces future result
- `FallbackIsNull` — null result is always safe (no crash invariant)
