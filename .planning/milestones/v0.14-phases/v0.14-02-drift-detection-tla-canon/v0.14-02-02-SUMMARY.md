## Decisions Made

- **esbuild external flag**: Plan specified `external: ['xstate']` but that causes `MODULE_NOT_FOUND` when the compiled bundle runs from /tmp (no node_modules). Deviated to bundle xstate inline — matches the `xstate-to-tla.cjs` pattern exactly. This is the correct approach for tmpdir bundles.
