### Broken Flows (1/7)

1. **Session End Learning Extraction — Skill Candidate Persistence** (LRNG-03)
   - Break point: `hooks/nf-session-end.js` line 124
   - `nf-session-end.js` calls `memStore.isDuplicate(cwd, 'skill_candidates', ...)` and `memStore.appendEntry(cwd, 'skill_candidates', ...)`
   - `memory-store.cjs` FILES map has 6 categories: `decisions`, `errors`, `quorum`, `corrections`, `skills`, `failures` — no `skill_candidates`
   - `getMemoryPath(cwd, 'skill_candidates')` resolves to `path.join(cwd, '.planning/memory', undefined)` → writes to `.planning/memory/undefined`
   - `nf-session-start.js` reads from `skills` category → extracted data never surfaces
   - **Fix:** Change `'skill_candidates'` to `'skills'` on lines 124-125, sync to `hooks/dist/`, re-run install
