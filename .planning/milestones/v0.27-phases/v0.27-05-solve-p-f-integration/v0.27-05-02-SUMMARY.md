## What Was Built

**bin/autoClosePtoF.cjs** — Two-track remediation dispatch:

1. **Parameter update track** (issue_type="drift" + numeric threshold): Transitions entry acknowledged->resolving, dispatches /qgsd:quick task, then transitions to resolved on success.

2. **Investigation track** (issue_type="issue" OR non-numeric formal_ref): Transitions to resolving, sets meta.investigation_notes, does NOT auto-dispatch.

3. **Freeze semantics**: Entries in resolving status are immune to concurrent observe overwrites per PF-04.

4. **Failed dispatch handling**: Entry stays in resolving (state machine does not allow resolving->acknowledged revert).

5. **Audit trail**: remediation_log and investigation_notes written to entry.meta.
