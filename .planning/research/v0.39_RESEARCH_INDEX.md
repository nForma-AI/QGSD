## Key Takeaways

1. **Dual-cycle is novel but well-founded.** No direct academic precedent, but synthesis of CEGAR + constraint mutation + invariant preservation is sound. Needs validation via Phase 02/03 research.

2. **Roadmap is ambitious but achievable.** 4 phases, ~10-14 weeks estimated (pending Phase 02/03 research). MVP is strong (7 table-stakes features + 3 convergence gates).

3. **Reuse v0.38 heavily.** Bug-model lookup, TLC execution, proximity-neighbor verification, inverted semantics all available. Minimize new infrastructure.

4. **Phase dependencies are strict.** 01 → 02 & 03 → 04. Sequential is safer than parallel. Partial overlap between 02 & 03 possible but risky.

5. **Two research blockers identified.** Phase 02 (Cycle 1 LLM explanation) and Phase 03 (Cycle 2 constraint DSL + mutation semantics) need ~5-8 days of validation before full commitment.
