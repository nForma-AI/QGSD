**SAFE-01 — --force bypass unreachable via /qgsd:health**

- **Guard works:** `regenerateState` at lines 3943-3957 fires correctly; `gsd-tools.cjs` binary at line 5366 parses `--force` correctly
- **Broken path:** `qgsd-core/workflows/health.md` `parse_args` step captures only `--repair`; `--force` is never forwarded to the binary call
- **Effect:** `/qgsd:health --repair --force` invocation leaves `--force` silently ignored; guard always fires on STATE.md >50 lines with no user-visible bypass
- **Workaround:** `node ~/.claude/qgsd/bin/gsd-tools.cjs validate health --repair --force` (direct CLI)
- **Fix required:** 2-line patch in `qgsd-core/workflows/health.md` + argument-hint update in `commands/qgsd/health.md` + install sync
